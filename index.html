Interpreting the results

Differences:

  • Bug: You're missing a bug release.
  • Feature: You're missing a minor / feature release.
  • Major: Oh no! You're missing a major release!
  • Error: Something went wrong while talking to PyPi.

Some stuff you should know:

  • This script is doing its best guess as to what you're missing. It's not a replacement for looking at release notes!
  • Most of the time, errors are due to PyPi redirects. For example, prettytable redirects to PrettyTable, and CORS freaks out.
  • Yes, you can contribute!
